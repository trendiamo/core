The importance of early product traction

Finding the right approach for you

Tommaso Di Stefano
Jan 8, 2019

From my personal experience, I can tell you that sitting down for one hour to think about how to promote your product in all these different channels is eye-opening! You are forced to think about your target and your product in very unconventional ways and these lessons you teach yourself are very valuable!
